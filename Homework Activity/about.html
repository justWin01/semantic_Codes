About Us

SCL Computer Store & Accessories was founded by Sherwin Cuevas Labe with a mission to provide high-quality, affordable computer components and accessories to every tech enthusiast and professional.

We specialize in a wide range of computer hardware—from processors, graphics cards, and memory, to accessories like keyboards, mice, and cables.

Our commitment is to deliver excellent customer service and the latest technology at competitive prices. Whether you're upgrading your system, building a new one, or maintaining your setup, SCL is your trusted tech partner.
